Who We Are

I'N I World Beat Culture was inspired by extensive travels across the globe, including Jamaica, Nigeria, Eritrea, the U.S., Russia, the DRC, and other regions such as Egypt, Morocco, Sudan, and Brazil. Combining these experiences with a love for Roots Rock and Reggae music, and Creole culture from Southern Louisiana, the concept was created to offer a unique restaurant and entertainment venue that embodies Rastafarian ideals of oneness. The venue provides a vibrant and welcoming atmosphere, featuring live music, tropical decor with lush plants and waterfalls, and semi-private seating booths with special lighting effects. The Kidane-Watson-inspired I'N I World Beat Culture aims to transport customers on a soulful journey of food, music, and culture, offering a truly immersive and unforgettable experience.
