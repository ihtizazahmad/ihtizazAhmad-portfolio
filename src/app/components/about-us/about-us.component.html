About Us

Discover the story behind Wraps Up

At Wraps Up, we believe that food doesn’t just nourish; it connects souls, cultures, and moments. Two years ago, in Atlanta, our dream was born: to transform a simple dish into an unforgettable experience. We wanted everyone to discover the flavors of the world in a practical and meaningful format.

Our Creations

We’re dedicated to something bigger than just making wraps. We create with purpose, with identity. Imagine tasting the flavors of a burger wrapped up in a wrap, with all the ingredients perfectly contained so they won’t fall out. Or enjoying the creamy comfort of Mac & Cheese with Chicken Tenders, all in a portable format that instantly comforts you. How about a Mexican Wrap, combining the vibrant flavors of tacos with the convenience of a wrap? Or a Pizza Wrap, where every bite tastes like a freshly baked slice. Our Caesar Chicken Grill Wrap is a favorite, blending freshness and texture in a single bite. Each creation is a tribute to cultural diversity, designed to delight and surprise.

Our Mission

We understand that time is a luxury, which is why we designed our wraps for those who need to eat well without slowing down. Our goal is not just to satisfy hunger, but to offer you a moment of happiness, a pause to reconnect with something special. We want you to enjoy a great meal, even on the busiest days.

Our Journey

Since our beginnings, we've received an incredible reception in Atlanta, where our wraps have become a favorite choice for those seeking quality and flavor. This success has inspired us to expand and bring our concept to Orlando, hoping to conquer more hearts and share our passion with a new community.

More Than Just Food

But Wraps Up is much more than just a place to grab a wrap. It’s an experience. With our themed catering stations—Italian, Mexican, American, and Venezuelan—and our live service, where wraps come straight off the grill, hot and fresh, we aim to make every event an unforgettable memory. We want to fill your celebrations with aromas, flavors, and moments that stay in your heart.

Our dream doesn’t stop here. We envision a vibrant space, a place where laughter and conversations blend with the aromas of the kitchen. A place where you can enjoy our wraps along with new dishes that will surprise your taste buds, accompanied by a lively bar and live music that makes every visit a celebration.

Join Us on Our Journey

At Wraps Up, each wrap tells a story. It’s a tribute to culture, a gift for your senses, and a way to connect with you. Because for us, it’s not just food. It’s love, it’s culture, it’s a little piece of the world in your hands.
