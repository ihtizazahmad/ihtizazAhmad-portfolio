At I,Ni Habesha, we believe food is more than sustenance—it’s a universal language that unites cultures, ignites memories, and uplifts the spirit. Inspired by global travels and rooted in the ideals of Rastafarian oneness, our journey began in Atlanta with a vision to fuse the flavors, rhythms, and energies of the world into one unforgettable experience. From the vibrant streets of Jamaica and Nigeria to the soulful vibes of Southern Louisiana and Eritrea, every dish and every sound tells a story. We invite you to explore a space where culinary artistry meets cultural expression—where the essence of Roots Rock, Reggae, and rainforest serenity comes alive through taste, sound, and ambiance.

Who We Are

The INI — I'N I World Beat Culture was conceptualized from travels and first-hand experiences of America’s best. This one-of-a-kind restaurant and entertainment venue embodies the Rastafarian ideals of oneness and offers an enthusiastic, peaceful, eclectic, diverse, and welcoming vibe that takes I'N I customers on a musical and culinary journey to different ports of call.

The Kidane inspiration for I'N I World Beat Culture stems from extensive travels and experiences in Jamaica, Nigeria, Eritrea, St. Louis MO, USA, Russia, the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC), Egypt, Morocco, Sudan, Brazil, and more. Having also studied in Southern Louisiana, the founders infused Creole culture into the experience. This global journey, combined with a passion for Roots Rock and Reggae music, led to the creation of a space that blends food, music, and ambiance.

I'N I World Beat Culture offers a wide range of entertainment, including live music from top performers in the Roots Rock and Reggae scene. The venue is designed like an authentic rainforest, with lush tropical plants, hanging florals, flowing waterfalls, and immersive lighting. Guests enjoy semi-private sitting booths ideal for meaningful social interaction.

The Kidane-Watson vision delivers an immersive, unforgettable experience. It’s a place where guests savor global cuisine, enjoy soulful music, and relax in an atmosphere that honors cultural diversity and artistic expression. Ladies and Gentlemen, this is The I’N I.

Our Creations

At I,Ni Habesha, our creations are more than meals—they are sensory journeys inspired by global travels and cultural encounters. Drawing influence from Jamaica, Nigeria, Eritrea, Brazil, Morocco, and beyond, every dish is designed to evoke the vibrant rhythms of Roots Rock and Reggae. We blend global street food traditions with innovative culinary craftsmanship, offering dishes that bring the world together in one bite. Whether it's a Creole-inspired wrap or an Ethiopian fusion bowl, our menu is a tribute to the beauty of cultural expression through food.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nourish not just the body, but also the spirit. We aim to create a vibrant, inclusive environment where guests experience unity through culture, cuisine, and music. Influenced by the Rastafarian ideal of oneness, our goal is to celebrate diversity and togetherness—offering every guest a space where they feel welcomed, inspired, and connected to something greater. Through flavorful creations and soulful ambiance, we strive to transform every meal into a meaningful experience.
